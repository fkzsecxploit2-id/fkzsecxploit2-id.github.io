Follow our official channel to receive the latest updates, important announcements, and new version releases of MashShell. On this channel.

Telegram Channels


Telegram channels are used for one-way information sharing, where admins provide the latest updates, news and important announcements. You can't send messages, but you'll always be notified of the latest developments.

Telegram Group


Telegram groups are used for two-way discussions, allowing all members to interact with each other, share experiences, ask questions and provide input. In this group, you can talk directly with admins and other users regarding MashShell.

WhatsApp Group


WhatsApp Group is an interactive discussion forum for MashShell users. Here, you can discuss directly with admins and other members, share information, provide feedback, and get support regarding using MashShell.
